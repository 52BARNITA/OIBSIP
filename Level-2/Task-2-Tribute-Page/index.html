Savitribai Phule

India’s First Female Teacher & Social Reformer

Early Life

Savitribai Phule was born in 1831 and became a pioneer in women’s education. She faced social opposition but remained committed to educating girls.

Contributions

  • First female teacher in India
  • Started the first girls’ school
  • Worked for women and social equality
  • Opposed caste and gender discrimination
“Awake, arise, and educate. Smash traditions—liberate!”
